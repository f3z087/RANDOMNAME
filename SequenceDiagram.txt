title Add Incoming Shipment Operation

Person->Interface: add shipment




Interface->+Shipment: getWarehouseID
Shipment-->-Interface: warehouseID


Interface->Warehouse: getWarehouseStatus

alt 
    Warehouse-->Interface: Freight receipt ended
    Interface->Person: Add shipment failed
else 
    Warehouse-->Interface: Freight receipt allowed
    Interface->Warehouse: addIncomingShipment
    Interface->Person: Add shipment successful
end
